ThinThread is a technical blog about models, evidence, and decisions. It follows the line from method to meaning.

I write about data science, machine learning, and AI systems. The focus is not model worship. The focus is what the result means, what decision changes, and where the interpretation can fail.

You will find posts on synthetic data and forecasting. You will also find posts on model interpretation, AI agents, and applied machine learning. Some posts are practical. Some are research-backed explainers. All of them try to answer the same downstream question: what should we believe or do next?

ThinThread is for readers who care about the work after the model runs.
